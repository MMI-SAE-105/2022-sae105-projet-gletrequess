Project Objective and Approach

This project was carried out in an educational setting in the MMI department of Montbéliard. In a more personal way, I myself chose a rather sinister theme and turned more towards human psychology. It is a subject that has always fascinated me and to associate it with written, musical and cinematographic works is a real opportunity to express this passion for this theme and this subject.
